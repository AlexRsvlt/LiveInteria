Meet the Designer


Atanu Biswas

Atanu Biswas, an accomplished artist and the owner of his company, is a passionate creator with a strong background in art and interiors. A graduate of Art College, Kolkata, he has dedicated his career to blending artistic expression with functional design. Formerly the Principal Photographer at The Times of India, Atanu's creative vision extends beyond photography into interior design and sculpture art.

In his early business years, he successfully built several mudhouses, both exteriors as well as interiors, marking the beginning of his innovative journey. With a relentless drive for excellence, he continues to push boundaries, delivering outstanding work in both interior and sculptural artistry.
